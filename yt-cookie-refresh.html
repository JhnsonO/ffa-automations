Install Get cookies.txt LOCALLY in Chrome. This is the standard tool for exporting YouTube cookies in the format yt-dlp needs.

Step 2 — Export YouTube cookies

Go to youtube.com and make sure you're logged in as footffa@gmail.com. Then click the extension icon and export cookies for the current site. Copy all the text.
